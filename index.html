F.A.Q

Frequently asked questions about bootcamp

  • Do I need to pay for the registration of the bootcamp ?

    No, it is free for everyone.

  • Is there any prerequisites required for the bootcamp ?

    No, everything will be covered from scratch (basics)

  • Will I get the participation certificate for attending the bootcamp ?

    Yes, but only to those members who will attend all the sessions.

  • Who all are eligible to win the exciting prizes/goodies ?

    The members who will both:
    1. Attend all the sessions of the bootcamp. And
    2. Winners of the challenge project and quiz after evaluation on the last day.
